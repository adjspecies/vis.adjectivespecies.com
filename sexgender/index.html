The data for these charts and the list below comes from the [adjective][species] survey on gender and sexuality. The freedom associated both with the anonymity of being on the internet and the process of living vicariously through a constructed persona allow a great deal deal of room for expression and exploration, perhaps beyond what we would do or is even possible outside our subculture.
