--- layout: default title: Years in the Fandom vs. Species ---

This graph shows the popularity of species over time by aggregating each species' year-to-year growth through responses from the furry survey, asking what species a respondent was as well as how many years they've been a part of the furry fandom. You may highlight a particular species by hovering over the species name below the visualization, or you may see which species a particular line belongs to by hovering over the line within the chart.
