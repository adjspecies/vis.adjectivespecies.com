The data for these charts and the list below comes from the [adjective][species] survey on monogamy and polygamy. There appears to be an expectation that furries will date and enter into relationships within the fandom, a relatively close-knit social group where many of the individuals share several interests in common. The intent of this survey is to judge the prevalence of monogamy and polygamy - that is, relationships that involve two, or relationships that involve more than two individuals - within the furry subculture. It asks questions regarding both mono- and poly-sexuality, and mono- and poly-romanticism in order to explore this topic.
